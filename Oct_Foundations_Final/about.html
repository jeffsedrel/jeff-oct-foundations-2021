About

I was born in Des Moines, Iowa, and raised in Omaha, Nebraska. I have a Bachelors degree in studio art from the University of Nebraska at Omaha, and have been cooking professionally for over a decade.

Recently I have gained an interest in coding. It comes from a desire to better understand the structure of the ever-changing internet, keep up with modren technology, and advance my long-term career prospects.

Outside of work I enjoy the outdoors, specifically running and cycling. I also like to travel. I love seeing new places and meeting new people!
